About

I'm a structural engineering professional and Engineer-in-Training based in the Los Angeles area — currently designing real structures as an intern at Hohbach-Lewin, Inc.

I'm pursuing my Master of Science in Structural Engineering at UC San Diego (expected 2027), building on bachelor's degrees in Structural Engineering at UC San Diego and Engineering Physics at Biola University through a 3+2 program. I passed the FE Civil exam in 2025 and earned my EIT in early 2026 — the first steps toward full PE/SE licensure.

My academic design work spans full steel and concrete buildings, and my internship has me deep in real wood-structure design — lateral systems, connections, and calculation packages. I care about sound load paths, buildable details, and clear communication. Good structure is rarely seen — but it's always felt.
