About

I'm a licensed structural engineer with over a decade of experience taking projects from concept through construction. My work spans commercial and residential buildings, structural investigations, and the kind of seismic and lateral-system design that keeps occupants safe when the ground moves.

I care about elegant load paths, buildable details, and clear communication with the architects, contractors, and owners I work alongside. Good structure is rarely seen — but it's always felt.

Expertise

Building Structures

Steel, concrete, masonry, and timber systems for new construction — gravity and lateral design from foundations to roof.

Seismic & Lateral Design

Performance-based and code-based seismic design, lateral force-resisting systems, and ductile detailing for high-demand regions.

Condition surveys, load-rating, and forensic investigation of existing buildings, plus retrofit and strengthening strategies.
