About

I'm a structural engineering professional and Engineer-in-Training (EIT) based in the Los Angeles area. I'm currently a structural engineering intern at Hohbach-Lewin Structural and Civil Engineers, where I model and analyze structures, design lateral force-resisting systems, and assemble calculation packages for real projects.

My path into engineering has been a wide one — from physics research and environmental engineering to hands-on structural design — and I'm pursuing dual bachelor's degrees in Structural Engineering at UC San Diego and Engineering Physics at Biola University. I passed the FE Civil exam in 2025 and earned my EIT in early 2026, the first steps toward full PE/SE licensure.

I care about sound load paths, buildable details, and clear communication with everyone on a project team. Good structure is rarely seen — but it's always felt.
