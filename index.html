About

I. KIM, EIT

I'm a Structural Engineering MS student at UCSD and an Engineer-in-Training at Hohbach-Lewin, Inc.

I am on track to complete my MS in Structural Engineering in the Spring of 2027, building on my BS in Structural Engineering from UCSD and BS in Engineering Physics from Biola University through a 3+2 program. I earned my Civil EIT in early 2026 and am planning on taking the PE Civil: Structural exam in early 2027, the next step to full licensure.

My academic breadth encompasses the design of steel and concrete structures (See Projects tab), and at Hohbach-Lewin I've learned to design timber structures as well, beginning with a design basis to submitting a final calculation package.

Outside of structures, I enjoy discussing philosophy, religion, technology, and am always looking to learn something new. "The unexamined life is not worth living" — Plato.
